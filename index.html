About

We will be inviting students from top universities (such as MIT, Tsinghua, and Peking University) or exceptionally talented high school students (e.g., MIT PRIMES, MOP, USAMO participants) to give short talks on interesting mathematical topics.

The goal of these talks is to broaden your mathematical horizons and inspire your interest in mathematics. The talks are designed to be engaging for participants of all mathematical backgrounds, with topics ranging from easier to more challenging, so that everyone can benefit.

We plan to hold these talks online, usually at convenient times on weekends.
